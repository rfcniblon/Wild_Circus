About Us

In a recent past, no one have believed that a circus withhout animals like elephants lions,monkeys, could be attractive.

The Wild Circus used animal since 1976 but decided to stop this pratice and the magic is always there on the countrary.

The name is to flight cruelty against animals in the industry. The Wild Circus's spectacles continue to show elephant, horse, circles of fire and acrobats, but for the first time in the world, none of the animals are real
